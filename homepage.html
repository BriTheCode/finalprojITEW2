Welcome To Bagong Cabuyao!

Cabuyao is a city in Laguna, a region in the Philippines. It covers the southern banks of Laguna de Bay, the largest lake in the country, and its rapidly increasing number of inhabitants now lies around 250,000.

Bahay ni Rizal

Bahay ni Rizal, also known as the Rizal Shrine, is the reconstructed ancestral house of José Rizal, the foremost national hero of the Philippines. Situated in Calamba, Laguna, this historic site provides a glimpse into the early life and background of the man who became a pivotal figure in the Filipino struggle for independence. Originally built in the 1840s, the house was where José Rizal was born on June 19, 1861.
