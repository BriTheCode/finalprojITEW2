Queen Aurora Resort

Queen Aurora Resorts is a popular resort located in Cabuyao, Laguna, Philippines. It offers a variety of amenities and attractions for visitors looking for a relaxing and enjoyable stay. Here’s an overview of Queen Aurora Resorts: Swimming Pools: Multiple swimming pools, including kiddie pools and adult pools. Water Slides: Fun and thrilling water slides for all ages. Wave Pool: A wave pool that simulates ocean waves.

South Pick Resort

South Pick Resort is a well-known resort located in Cabuyao, Laguna, Philippines, popular for its serene environment and extensive facilities. Here’s a detailed description of South Pick Resort: Location: Purok 2, Brgy. Banay-Banay, Cabuyao, Laguna, Philippines. Type: Family-oriented resort offering both day tours and overnight accommodations. Rooms and Cottages: Various types of rooms and cottages designed for couples, families, and groups, featuring modern amenities and comfortable furnishings. Bungalows and Villas: Private bungalows and villas with added amenities for a more luxurious experience. Dormitory-Style Rooms: Available for larger groups or budget-conscious travelers.

Rising Sun Hot Spring Resort

Rising Sun Hot Spring Resort in Cabuyao, Laguna, is a charming destination known for its natural hot springs and relaxing ambiance. Here’s an in-depth description of the resort: Location: Purok 4, Brgy. Banay-Banay, Cabuyao, Laguna, Philippines. Type: Hot spring resort offering day tours and overnight stays, with an emphasis on relaxation and wellness. Standard Rooms: Basic yet comfortable rooms equipped with essential amenities such as air conditioning, private bathrooms, and TVs. Family Rooms: Larger rooms designed for families or groups, offering more space and additional amenities. Suites: More luxurious options with enhanced features such as private hot spring tubs and garden views.
